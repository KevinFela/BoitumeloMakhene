My Journey

My journey in the entertainment industry started with a simple love for connecting with people and creating memorable experiences. Over time, I discovered my talent for reading crowds and creating the perfect atmosphere for any event.

From small local gigs to major festivals, I've honed my skills as both an MC and club host, learning how to adapt to different audiences and event types. My approach is always professional, but with that personal touch that makes each event feel special.

As FuglyMammie, I'm not just a performer - I'm a brand that represents quality entertainment and unforgettable experiences. I'm constantly evolving and looking for new ways to grow both as an entertainer and as a businesswoman.
